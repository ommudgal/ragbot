FoundationStoneLayingCeremonybyHon’bleChiefMinister

TheUPgovernment’sOneDistrict,OneProduct(ODOP)Programmeaimstoimprovedevelopmentandproductionofindigenousandspecializedproducts.GhaziabadisidentifiedasahubformanufacturingofGeneralEngineeringProducts.Manyofthesemanufacturersaresmallandmediumindustriesthatneedmodernization,machineryandproductivityenhancement.
AKGECinitsendeavourtopromoteIndustryInstituteInteraction,hasinitiatedtheproposalandgotapprovalfromDepartmentofMSME&ExportPromotionfortheestablishmentofCommonFacilityCenterforGeneralEngineeringIndustryunder“OneDistrictOneProduct(ODOP)”schemeofGovt.ofUPatAKGECCampus,Ghaziabad
Toimplementthisscheme,“AKGFoundationforInnovationandProductDevelopment”hasbeenincorporatedasaconsortiumof22IndustriesandAcademia,includingsomeoftheprominentIndustriesofthisregion.
ThisCFCtobeestablishedatAjayKumarGargEngineeringCollegewillhaveaHighEndEngineeringProductDevelopmentFacilityforLocalIndustriesincludingModernToolRoom,MaterialTestingLab,Research&DevelopmentLabandIndustry4.0Lab.
TheFoundationStoneLayingCeremony(Shilanyas)ofCommonFacilityCentreforGeneralEngineeringIndustryofGhaziabad,hasbeenconductedtodayinonlinemodebySh.YogiAdityanathji,Hon'bleChiefMinisterofUttarPradesh,inthebenignpresenceofSh.SiddharthNathSinghji,Hon'bleCabinetMinister,MSME&ExportPromotion,ShChaudharyUdaybhanSinghjiHon'bleStateMinister,MSME&ExportPromotionandShNavneetSehgalji,IAS,AdditionalChiefSecretary,DepttofMSME&ExportPromotion.
ThisceremonywasheldatAKGECCampusandattendedbyHon'bleMinisterShriAtulGargjiwithHon'bleDistrictMagistrateofGhaziabadShri.RakeshKumarSingh(IAS)andShri.BirendraKumar,Jt.Commissioner,IndustriesandothereminentAdministrativeAuthoritiesincludingAKGECManagementShRakeshGargji,Sh.VinayGargjiandSh.AdiNarayanji,ShR.PChadhajiandShSubhashGargjiwiththeSPVMembers:Sh.AmitAgarwalji,ShKrishnaAvtarGargji,ShParasSharmaji,ShSushilChawalaji.
DuringtheprogramDr.R.K.Agarwal,CEO,AKGFoundationforInnovation&ProductDevelopmentpresenteddetailsoftheProjecttoHon'bleChiefMinisterandconveyedgratitudeonbehalfofSPVmembersforsanctionofthismuchneededCFCforGhaziabadIndustry.TheHon'bleChiefMinisterconveyedhisbestwishesforsuccessfulimplementationofthisprojectandachievingthedesiredobjectivesofCFC.
