MBA Admission 2023-25 






MBA 2023-25 (AKGIM College Code: 820)

Click here for MBA Admission 2023-25
Master of Business Administration (MBA) 2021-23 is offered at AKGIM (AKTU College Code: 820). The course is affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow and approved by All India Council for Technical Education, MHRD, New Delhi.
For admission to MBA first year under direct mode, a candidate should have completed his/her graduation and must meet the minimum eligibility criteria as laid down by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and the AKTU Lucknow. Candidates appearing in the final year/semester of their graduation also can apply.
Admission to MBA program is done through UPTAC counselling based on CUET Scores as conducted by the National Testing Agency and through direct mode at the college level.
Admission, however, depends on the competence of the candidates and shall be subject to the availability of seats.
