Training Program GPP 





The initiation ceremony on 12th July 2023 marked the official start of the program, setting the stage for GPP participants to embark on their journey of acquiring essential knowledge and skills in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The program will run for a duration of three months until 31st October 2023.
Facilitated by the Department of EN and AKGEC Skills at the college, this program focuses on providing individuals with fundamental knowledge and practical skills in the field. Participants will have the opportunity to explore various aspects of the industry, including Electrical Test & Measuring Instruments, Supply Systems, Circuits & Control, Electrical Machines, Converters, Batteries, and Protection.
AKGEC places high priority on continuous learning and professional growth. This training program serves as an excellent platform for participants to enhance their expertise in the field, regardless of their experience level.
Aim of this training is to provide valuable insights and practical skills that will significantly contribute to the participants’ career development.
